What these settings mean:
  • Top N: number of highest‑strength runners we allow into the permutations. Lower values produce fewer lines and a more concentrated staking plan; higher values produce more coverage and a longer tail of lines.
  • Coverage: fraction of ranked lines you “buy” starting from the highest probability. For example 0.60 buys the top 60% of lines by model probability.
  • Hit Rate: share of races where the true 1–4 finishing order is within the purchased coverage. It does not account for EV or stake sizing; use the calculators/viability page for that.
  • Weights: the model uses predictions when present; otherwise it derives strengths from WIN probable odds (mapped to horse IDs). This is the same weighting engine used by the calculator on the Superfecta page.
